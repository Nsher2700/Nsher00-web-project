Amadeus: My Favorite Movie

Amadeus is my favorite movie because it mixes drama, music, and history in a really unique way. Instead of just showing Mozart’s life, it focuses on how his talent affects the people around him, especially Salieri.

What makes the movie interesting is that Mozart isn’t shown as perfect. He’s talented, but also immature and reckless at times. That contrast makes his character feel more real.

Salieri’s perspective is what really drives the story. He recognizes Mozart’s genius but struggles with jealousy because he knows he can never reach that level. That conflict makes the movie feel intense.

Why I Like Amadeus

  • Strong emotional story about jealousy and talent
  • Memorable acting and characters
  • Incredible use of classical music
  • Unique storytelling perspective
