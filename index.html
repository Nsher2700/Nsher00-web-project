Welcome to My Website

This website is about my favorite movie, Amadeus. It’s a film that tells the story of Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart through the perspective of his rival, Antonio Salieri.

I picked this movie because it’s not just about music — it’s about jealousy, talent, and how people deal with feeling overshadowed by others. It makes the story feel way more intense than a normal biography.
